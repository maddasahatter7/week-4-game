Star Catcher

  1. At the start of each game, you will be presented with a random number.
  2. There are 4 different types of star below. Click one to raise your score by the stars assigned value.
  3. To win the game, you must match your score to the value of the random number.
  4. Watch out! The game will be lost if your score exceeds the value of the random number!
  5. The value of each star will be hidden until you click it.
  6. After each game, each star will be assigned a new hidden value and the random number will change.

Good Luck and happy star catching!
